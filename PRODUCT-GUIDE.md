# SquidRun Product Guide

Welcome to SquidRun! This guide covers key features and capabilities of your multi-model agent team.

## Cross-Device Pairing

You can connect two SquidRun instances running on different machines (for example, a desktop and a laptop). This allows you to orchestrate your agents across multiple devices.

### Requirements
- Both devices must have internet access.

### How to use it
1. On your first device, open **Settings > Devices**.
2. Click to generate a new pairing code.
3. On your second device, open **Settings > Devices** and enter the pairing code.
4. The two instances will securely connect!

*For further details on agent architecture and technical setup, please see the `README.md`.*
